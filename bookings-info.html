FAQs

1. What is Crystal Xrbia?

Crystal Xrbia is a JV between the Crystal Group & XRBIA Developers.

Crystal Group with over 36 years of experience, is India’s fastest growing real estate developer with a major presence in Mumbai across residential, commercial and retail projects. With successful completion of 1.6 million sq. ft. of projects & 4 million sq. ft. of planned construction in Mumbai, Crystal Group has proved to be an expert in sophisticated development in the real estate sector.

XRBIA is India's leader in affordable housing with a sale & build rate of 2000 houses a month. It is ready to change the traditional way in which housing has been defined across the world. XRBIA is embarking on a new journey that lays the benchmark for quality and modern urban development in the country.

Yes, you will be able to avail a refund on the Registration amount paid i.e. Rs. 100.

9. Will I get Refund of the Booking?

Refund after booking is not provided. If you fail to make the down payment after paying the booking amount, the Rs. 50,000 is non-refundable.

10. Are there any Government Subsidies available?

Customers may avail a central government subsidy of Rs.2.5 lacs for affordable housing under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yogna for families with household income below Rs.18 lacs. You may read the complete details at:

http://www.crystalxrbia.com/subsidy.html

11. How do I go about the Booking Process?

Prior to booking, be sure to register by 20th April. Bookings start on 21st of April. Registered applicants will be contacted on a first come first served basis. Customers can select the flat online with a non-refundable booking amount of Rs.50,000. Applicants can book the flat of their choice and select their preferred financing option. For assistance, customers can also visit the site or speak to our call center. Once the booking process is complete customers will receive a receipt and confirmation letter.

12. How are the flats allotted?

Within 7 days of booking, customers are required to pay the balance down payment amount based on their selected financial scheme. Customers will receive the Allotment letter once this payment is done.
